Latitude vs Wind Speed

In the above graph, please note the axes. On the x-axis is Latitude, ranging from -80 to +100 degrees. On the y-axis is the wind speed the day the data was acquired, in miles per hour. One pattern that arises here is the highest wind speeds recorded (over 25 mph) are in the range of 50-70 degrees North. Is it possible that wind speeds are greatest in the Northern Hemisphere? It's hard to say: our sample was a snapshot of one day, and to test the hypothesis, one would need a larger sample of days.
