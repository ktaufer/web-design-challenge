Latitude vs Humidity

On the x-axis is Latitude, ranging from -80 to +100 degrees. On the y-axis is the percent humidity the day the API was queried. There seems to be a fairly equal sampling of humidity levels at each latitude, it seems humidity is more dependent on terrain than latitude. It is interesting that the only places that had no humidity at all were in the Northern Hemisphere, in the range of 60-70 degrees North. Sadly, we have no data points as far south to compare, since there is so little land mass in the Southern Hemisphere.
